About Application

Welcome to R & M MOVIE ZONE . This is an AI based web application in which you can search for any Hollywood Movie. This application will provide all the information related to that movie, does sentiment analysis on the movie reviews and the most interesting part, this application will provide you the top 10 movie recommendations based on your search.
